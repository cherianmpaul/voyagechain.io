Questions

What exactly does a VCT token give me?
+
A VCT token is your lifetime cruise pass. It entitles you to board any VoyageChain ship, on any available voyage, completely free of charge — forever. There are no annual renewals, no booking fees, and no expiry dates. The token is a prepaid service contract, not a financial instrument.
How does the Priority System work exactly?
+
Every VCT holder can cruise for free — the priority system only determines when your booking window opens for a given voyage. Holders of 500+ VCT can book 180 days in advance and choose from all cabins. Holders of less then 1 VCT can book 30 days in advance from remaining inventory. All bookings are processed by smart contract — it reads your wallet balance at booking time, opens the appropriate window, and issues your NFT boarding pass instantly.
Is VCT a cryptocurrency investment or security?
+
No. VCT is a utility token — a prepaid cruise service contract. When you buy a VCT token, you are purchasing access to a cruise service, not making a financial investment. VoyageChain makes no promises of financial return, yield, dividends, or token price appreciation. The token gives you one thing: the right to sail. This structure is comparable to purchasing a lifetime gym membership or airline miles, and has been designed in consultation with legal counsel to comply with applicable regulations across major jurisdictions.
Can I sell or transfer my VCT tokens?
+
Yes. VCT tokens are standard ERC-20 tokens held in your own wallet. You may sell, transfer, or gift them at any time on the secondary market. There is no platform lock-in and no custodian holding your tokens. Your tokens — your rules.
Why will there only ever be one billion VCT tokens?
+
The one-billion hard cap is permanently enforced at the smart contract level — it cannot be changed by any person, including VoyageChain's founders. This creates genuine, irreversible scarcity. As the fleet grows and demand for cruise access increases, the fixed supply means tokens in circulation become increasingly valuable as access passes. VoyageChain holds the vast majority of tokens in its treasury, releasing them slowly to fund fleet expansion over decades.
When will the public token sale launch?
+
The public token sale date has not yet been announced. VoyageChain is currently in the Founding Investor round (Round 1), which is an invitation-only round for institutional investors. The public sale will follow. Joining the waitlist is the best way to receive early notification and secure your position ahead of the general public.
Where will VoyageChain ships sail?
+
VoyageChain's vision is to operate a global fleet covering all major ocean routes on six continents — Mediterranean, Caribbean, North Atlantic, Pacific, Indian Ocean, Arctic, and Antarctic. Phase 1 launches with partner-operated voyages on established routes. As the owned fleet grows through Phases 2–4, route diversity expands significantly. Token holders will have access to every VoyageChain voyage, on every ship, on every route — free, always.
